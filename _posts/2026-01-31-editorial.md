It is the beginning of a new year again, and with it comes the sense that something is starting over, even if we are only continuing from where we left off. This time, that feeling also comes with the launch of the new version of Sahyadri Connect.
Along with everything else happening this term, the website has been redesigned over the past few weeks. The process was not smooth. There was confusion, experimentation, and a lot had to be reworked more than once. But slowly, it began to take shape.
Sahyadri Connect has always been a space meant to bring different parts of the school together—students, teachers, alumni and parents. While working on it, one question kept coming back. How do you make something people will actually return to, and not just visit once and forget? Most of what has changed comes from trying to answer that.
The intention was to make the website feel like a place alumni can come back to. Not just to check something, but to pause for a moment and remember. The kind of place that holds traces of the more carefree and uncomplicated parts of being here. At the same time, it still needed to function as a school platform. That balance shaped the way it looks and feels. It is meant to be simple, warm, and open, rather than something that feels too formal or distant, even in sections that deal with more official information.
It is not perfect, and it does not need to be. It just needs to be something people feel comfortable using and contributing to. Something that reflects the school in an honest way.
Seeing it finally out there feels different. It is no longer just something on my screen. What it becomes now depends on the people who use it and what they choose to make of it.
For now, it is just the beginning. In this issue, and in the ones to come, you can expect these newsletters to find a place on the website, something to return to whenever you want to revisit them.
