Freshers’ Eve is known to be one of the most anticipated, fun-filled events of the new school year. All freshers look forward to showcasing their talents, either individually or with their friends. This year, Freshers’ Eve was held on 12th July, from 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m.

#### A Diverse Lineup
There was a unique lineup of performances that evening, including solving four Rubik’s Cubes in under two minutes, group and solo song covers, poetry, guitar, and much more, including featuring ballet for the first time on the Sahyadri Stage. The anchors for the event were senior members of the cultural committee, along with some new faces.

#### Highlights and Success
The event began with a vibrant gymnastics display and ended with a thrilling band performance. Overall, Freshers’ Eve was a resounding success, warmly welcoming the newcomers to the school and giving them the opportunity and platform to present their talents in the performing arts.
